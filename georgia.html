Hiking

Most of the hiking trails lie within 2000 m above the sea and surround
famous for their beauty villages, such as Keseloebi old towers or Kvavlo fortress.
Innumerable long trails will walk you around the beauty and open the whole new world to you.
During this period most of the trails are open and accessible, so prepare well now.
